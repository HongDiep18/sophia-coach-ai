# Settings

The Settings page (`/settings`) is where you customize your learning experience.
It has three groups of options: English Level, Speech, and Preferences.

## English Level
This sets the difficulty of the coach's English and controls how complex the
vocabulary in its answers is. You can choose:
- **A2 — Elementary** — simpler words and shorter sentences.
- **B1 — Intermediate** — everyday, moderate English.
- **Technical — Developer English** — English aimed at software developers.

## Speech (text-to-speech)
This controls how spoken replies sound. The **Speech rate** slider sets the
reading speed, from 0.5x (slow) to 1.5x (fast). Slower speeds help you hear every
syllable clearly, which is useful when you are learning pronunciation.

## Preferences
Two on/off switches:
- **Auto-speak AI responses** — when on, the coach's replies are read aloud
  automatically instead of only when you press the speaker button.
- **Show Vietnamese translation** — when on, chat replies show the Vietnamese
  translation under the English (bilingual view).

## Saving your settings
Your changes are **not** saved automatically. Press the **Save Settings** button
at the bottom to save them; you will see a "Settings saved!" confirmation. Your
settings are stored in your browser on the device you are using.

## Default settings
If you have never changed anything, the app starts with: level **B1**, speech
rate **0.75x**, Vietnamese translation **on**, and auto-speak **off**.
