# Overview

## What is Sophia Coach AI?
Sophia Coach AI (shown inside the app as **FluentFlow — AI English Coach**) is
an app that helps you practice and improve your English with an AI coach. You
can practice by speaking out loud or by typing in a chat, and the coach replies
with corrections, tips, and a Vietnamese translation so you always understand.

## Who is it for?
It is built for English learners, especially Vietnamese speakers and software
developers who want to improve their spoken and written English for work. The
coach is designed to talk about real developer topics like projects, interviews,
and technical words, and it supports levels from A2 (elementary) up to C1.

## What can it do? (main features)
- **Voice Assistant** — speak English and get a spoken reply. See `voice-assistant.md`.
- **Chat** — type to the coach and get a written reply with translation and tips. See `chat.md`.
- **Vocabulary Bank** — save and review words you want to learn. See `vocabulary.md`.
- **Settings** — set your level, speech speed, and preferences. See `settings.md`.

## Why use it?
You get instant, patient feedback in both English and Vietnamese, so you can
practice speaking and writing English every day without needing a human tutor.

## How do I get started?
Open the app and pick how you want to practice. The home page is the Voice
Assistant for speaking practice; use the top menu to go to Chat, Vocab, or
Settings. A blue chat button also floats in the bottom-right corner of every
page (except the Chat page itself) — tap it any time to open the coach chat.

## Getting around the app
The top navigation bar has four sections: **Voice Assistant** (the home page),
**Chat**, **Vocab**, and **Settings**. The floating blue robot button in the
bottom-right corner is a shortcut that always takes you to the Chat page.
