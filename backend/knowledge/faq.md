# FAQ

Short answers to common questions about the app.

## What is Sophia Coach AI?
It is an AI English coach app (shown in the app as FluentFlow) where you practice
English by speaking or typing, and get replies with corrections, tips, and a
Vietnamese translation. See the overview for more.

## What languages does it support?
You practice in **English**, and the coach also gives a **Vietnamese**
translation of its replies and word meanings, so Vietnamese speakers can always
follow along.

## What is the difference between Chat and the Voice Assistant?
The **Voice Assistant** is for speaking practice: you talk and the coach talks
back out loud. **Chat** is for typing: you write and the coach replies in text,
with a translation, tips, and clickable words you can look up and save.

## Do I need a microphone?
Only for the **Voice Assistant**, which needs a microphone and your permission to
use it. The **Chat** page works with just your keyboard, no microphone needed.

## Which browser should I use for the Voice Assistant?
Use **Google Chrome on desktop** or **Chrome on Android**. Some other browsers do
not support the speech recognition the Voice Assistant needs. If yours doesn't,
the app will tell you and you can use Chat instead.

## My microphone isn't working — what should I do?
Make sure you are using Chrome, that a microphone is connected, and that you
allowed microphone access when the browser asked. If you accidentally blocked it,
allow microphone permission for the site in your browser and try again.

## How do I save a word to my Vocabulary Bank?
In Chat, tap any word in the coach's reply to open the word lookup window, then
press **Save to Vocab**. The word is added to your Vocabulary Bank with the
status "New".

## How do the learning statuses work?
Saved words move through three statuses: **New**, **Learning**, and **Mastered**.
Press **Next Status** on a word card to move it forward through those stages to
track your progress.

## Can I change how hard the coach's English is?
Yes. Go to **Settings** and change your **English Level** (A2, B1, or Technical
for developer English). This controls how complex the coach's vocabulary is.

## Can the app read replies out loud?
Yes. Tap the speaker icon on any chat reply to hear it, adjust the **speech rate**
slider to change the speed, or turn on **Auto-speak AI responses** in Settings to
have replies read automatically.

## How do I hide or show the Vietnamese translation?
In Chat, use the eye icon to toggle the translation on or off. You can also set
**Show Vietnamese translation** in Settings.

## Where are my saved words and settings stored?
They are stored in your browser on the device you are using. This means they stay
on that device and are not automatically shared with other browsers or computers.

## Ứng dụng này hỗ trợ tiếng Việt không?
Có. Bạn luyện tập bằng **tiếng Anh**, và huấn luyện viên AI sẽ kèm theo bản dịch
**tiếng Việt** cho câu trả lời và nghĩa của từ, nên người nói tiếng Việt luôn có
thể hiểu được.

## Tôi cần micro để dùng ứng dụng không?
Chỉ cần micro cho phần **Voice Assistant** (luyện nói). Phần **Chat** (nhắn tin)
chỉ cần bàn phím, không cần micro.
