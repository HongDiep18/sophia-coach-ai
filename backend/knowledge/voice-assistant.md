# Voice Assistant

The Voice Assistant is the home page of the app (the `/` page). It lets you
practice **speaking** English out loud and hear the coach reply back to you.

## What it does
You press the microphone button and speak in English. The app listens and shows
what you said as a live transcript. When you finish, your words are sent to the
coach, and the coach's answer appears on screen and is read out loud. The reply
streams in sentence by sentence, so you hear the first sentence quickly instead
of waiting for the whole answer.

## How to use it (step by step)
1. Choose your **Level** (A2, B1, B2, or C1) from the dropdown at the top.
2. Press **Start mic** and begin speaking in English.
3. Watch your words appear under "You said (transcript)".
4. Press **Stop mic** when you finish. Your message is sent automatically.
5. Read and listen to the coach's answer under "Coach answer".
6. If you prefer, press **Send** to submit the captured text manually.

## Hearing the answer again
After the coach replies, a **Replay answer** button appears. Press it to hear the
full answer read aloud again. Playback is spoken at a slightly slower speed so it
is easy to follow.

## Which browsers work?
The Voice Assistant uses your browser's built-in speech recognition, which works
in **Google Chrome on desktop** or **Chrome on Android**. If your browser does
not support it, the app shows a message and you can use the typed **Chat** page
instead.

## Do I need a microphone or permissions?
Yes. You need a working microphone, and you must allow the browser to use your
microphone when it asks. If you deny permission, speaking will not work and you
will see a microphone error.

## The connection indicator
There is a small status badge that shows the live connection to the coaching
service: it reads "disconnected", "connecting", or "connected". A "connected"
status means your streaming reply is ready to come through.

## Tips for best results
Speak clearly at a normal pace in a quiet place. Tap the mic again to stop as
soon as you finish your sentence, so your message is sent right away.
