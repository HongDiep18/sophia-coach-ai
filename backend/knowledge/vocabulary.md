# Vocabulary Bank

The Vocabulary page (`/vocabulary`) is your **Vocabulary Bank** — the collection
of English words you have saved to learn and review.

## What it does
It shows your saved words as cards. Each card displays the word, its meaning, an
example sentence, and a colored status label showing how well you know it. At the
top you can see how many words you have saved in total and how many are in each
status.

## How words get added
You add words from the **Chat** page: tap a word in the coach's reply to open the
word lookup window, then press **Save to Vocab**. New words are saved with the
status **New**. The app also starts with a few example words (such as "scalable",
"refactor", and "deploy") so the page is not empty the first time you open it.

## Learning statuses
Each word has one of three statuses, shown by color:
- **New** (grey) — a word you just saved and have not studied yet.
- **Learning** (amber) — a word you are actively practicing.
- **Mastered** (green) — a word you know well.

## Changing a word's status
Press the **Next Status** button on a card to move it forward. The status cycles
in order: New → Learning → Mastered, and then back to New. This lets you track
your progress as you learn each word.

## Finding and filtering words
Use the search box to find a word by its spelling or its meaning. Use the filter
buttons — **All**, **New**, **Learning**, **Mastered** — to show only words in a
certain status.

## Deleting a word
Press the **Delete** button on a card to remove that word from your bank.

## Where are my saved words kept?
Your saved words are stored in your browser on the device you are using. This
means they stay on that device and are not shared across different browsers or
computers.
