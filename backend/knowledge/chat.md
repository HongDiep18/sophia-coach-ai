# Chat Coaching

The Chat page (`/chat`) lets you practice English by **typing**. You send a
message and the AI coach — an English speaking coach for software developers —
replies in writing.

## What it does
When you send a message, the coach replies with four things:
- an **English** reply (2–4 short, friendly sentences),
- a **Vietnamese translation** of that reply,
- a short **analysis** — one tip to improve your English, and
- a few **suggestions** — ready-made reply options you can tap to continue.

## How to use it (step by step)
1. Type your message in the input box at the bottom and send it.
2. Wait a moment while "Coach is typing..." shows.
3. Read the coach's English reply, with the Vietnamese translation below it.
4. Tap **Show tips & suggestions** to see the improvement tip and reply options.
5. Tap any suggestion chip to send it as your next message and keep practicing.

## Hearing a reply out loud
Each coach reply has a small speaker icon. Tap it to have the English read aloud.
You can change the reading speed with the speech-rate slider in the top controls.

## Showing or hiding the Vietnamese translation
Use the eye icon in the top controls to toggle the Vietnamese translation on or
off, so you can test your understanding with English only when you feel ready.

## Looking up and saving a word
Every word in the coach's English reply is clickable. Tap a word to open the
**word lookup** window, which shows its part of speech, pronunciation (UK and US,
each with a listen button), an English definition, the Vietnamese meaning, and an
example sentence. Press **Save to Vocab** to add the word to your Vocabulary Bank.

## Starting over
Press **New Chat** to clear the conversation and start a fresh practice session.

## When should I use Chat vs. the Voice Assistant?
Use **Chat** when you want to practice writing, read the reply carefully, look up
words, and save vocabulary. Use the **Voice Assistant** when you want to practice
speaking and listening out loud.
